Algarve

Portimão

Algarve is a region of exceptional natural heritage and portimao is the city of one of the Algarve’s most famous beaches: Praia da Rocha.

Walking along the ciffs to the west you will find the beach of the Three Castles, Praia do Vau and Praia do Alemão. In Vau it´s possible to climb the cliffs and hike the tracks that provide a fantastic view over the sea.
